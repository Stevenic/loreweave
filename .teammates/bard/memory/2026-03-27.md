## Task: Fix Saving Throws Mixed Into Skill Checks (Loremaster Feedback)

- **What:** Loremaster's Round 2 review flagged that saving throws were mixed into `skill_checks` arrays in several location archetypes. In D&D 5e, saving throws and ability checks use different resolution rules and proficiency tracking — they must be separate.
- **Schema change:** Added `saving_throws` field to `challenges` in `archetype_format.md`. Uses `ability_save_description` naming convention (e.g., `wisdom_save_resist_enchantment`).
- **Files changed:** All 22 dungeon + wilderness location JSONs in `worlds/shared/locations/`, plus `archetype_format.md`
- **Saves moved out of skill_checks:**
  - fairy_ring: `wisdom_save_resist_enchantment`
  - haunted_manor: `wisdom_save_resist_fear`
  - frozen_lake: `constitution_save_resist_cold_exposure`
  - wizard_tower: `dexterity_save_avoid_magical_surge`
  - swamp_hollow: `constitution_save_resist_swamp_fever`
  - sewer: `constitution_save_endure_stench` (also caught — was `constitution_endure_stench` without `_save_` prefix)
- **17 remaining files:** Got empty `saving_throws: []` for schema consistency
- **Naming convention established:** `ability_save_description` for saves vs `skill_action` for checks
